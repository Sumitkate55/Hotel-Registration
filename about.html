About Hotel Orient

Hotel Orient is a cherished family-owned restaurant located in the heart of Pune. We specialize in bringing traditional recipes to life with a modern, fresh twist. Our mission is to create a welcoming and cozy atmosphere where every meal feels like a celebration. Our chefs, trained in both classic and contemporary techniques, use only the freshest local ingredients to craft a truly unique dining experience.

Our commitment to quality extends beyond our food. We pride ourselves on exceptional service and a beautiful dining environment, with both indoor and "outdoor" seating options to suit your mood. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a meal with loved ones, Little Lemon is the perfect place to create lasting memories.
